402 South 3rd Street West

· Owner: Dr. E.C. Prall

· Age: 1875

· Brick Pattern: English Garden Wall

· Architectural Style: Greek Revival characteristics. This small, nearly square home has the door on the right side of the house with a window which is almost the height of the door, a trend which appears later in the 19th Century. Above the door and window with shutters, the woodwork with a Parthenon pediment motif forms the lintels.
