McWethy Hall (Alumni Gym)

Address: Cornell College Campus
District: Cornell College
Year Built: 1909
Builder: Grant Miller
Architect: Grant Miller
Owner: Cornell College
Use: P.E. & gymnasium facility
Physical Description:
Style-
Construction Type-Rectangle with 2 projecting, full height mass (bsement, entries, parapet)

Features:

¨ Hip roof, terracotta, tin ridge protector
¨ Walls are brick with quoins style corners
¨ Limestone horizontal banding at 1st floor and below eave line and on parapet
¨ Windows on 1st floor are 2 pane with rectangle frame set in full arch of brick with keystone
¨ Windows on 2nd floor are 2 pane with rectangle frame with linestone and sills-jack arch lintel
¨ Single pane skylight on 3rd floor
¨ No windows on north façade 1st and 2nd floors
¨ Entrance mass highly ornate brick arch with radiating pattern
¨ 2nd floor window is leaded glass, limestone sill, jack arch

History/Comments:

The original porch included skylights and was replaced by composition shingles in 1959.
