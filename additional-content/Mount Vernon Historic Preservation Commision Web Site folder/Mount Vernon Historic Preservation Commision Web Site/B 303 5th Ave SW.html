303 South 5th Avenue West

· Owner: Cornell College

· Age: c. 1865

· Brick Pattern: English Garden Wall

· Architectural Style: Colonial tendencies predominate the style of the home. Three windows on the front in the second story with a central doorway and one window on the right side with the one of the other side having been filled in with brick. There is a porch and an addition, which were not on the original home.
